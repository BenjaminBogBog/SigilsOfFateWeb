A turn-based 1v1 battler where mythological heroes clash using runes drawn from a shuffle bag. Strategically place runes to form unique shapes—like Tetris—to trigger powerful abilities. Each hero has distinct valid shapes and game-changing effects, making every match a mind-bending duel of wits and combos!

Characters

Hypnos
Greek god of sleep and dreams. Hypnos relies on slows and speed stats to make his enemies unable to play slowly killing his foe.

Phraya Nak
Phraya Nak, a water serpent who guards sacred places. They say the more heads she has the more powerful she is. She is a tank relying on heals to keep her alive while enemies exhaust themselves

Fenrir
Fenrir, the monstrous wolf born of Loki and Angrboða, is destined to bring about Ragnarok with his untamed fury. Bound by the gods in chains, he waits for the day he will break free, unleashing destruction and devouring Odin himself.
